        Mentor advice

        Do not chase the perfect field. Chase curiosity.

        Pick a domain by trying small projects, not by guessing. If you enjoy making screens, try frontend. If you enjoy logic and data, try backend or data science. If you enjoy puzzles, try security.

        Explore before deciding because most beginners only know a tiny part of tech at first. It is okay to switch later. Your early goal is to learn fundamentals: variables, loops, functions, problem solving, and how to break big problems into small steps.

        Start with Python, build tiny tools, then try data, backend, AI, or automation.
